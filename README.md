# Guess the Word — Discord Activity

A Wordle-style word-guessing game playable directly inside Discord. Every day, a new **5-letter word** is chosen — the same word for everyone. You have **5 attempts** to guess it. Color-coded feedback guides you after each guess:

- 🟩 **Green** — Correct letter, correct position
- 🟨 **Yellow** — Letter is in the word, but wrong position  
- ⬛ **Gray** — Letter is not in the word at all

One game per day. Come back tomorrow for a new word!

---

## Features

- **Daily word** — One shared word per calendar day for all players
- **5 attempts** — Tighter and more exciting than classic Wordle
- **Keyboard support** — Type with your physical keyboard or click the on-screen keys
- **Shake animation** — Invalid words shake to let you know immediately
- **Flip reveal** — Letters flip to reveal their color after each guess
- **Confetti** — A celebratory burst when you find the word
- **Statistics** — Tracks your play count, win rate, current streak, and guess distribution
- **Playing now** — Shows friends who are in the same voice channel/DM activity instance

---

## How to Play on Discord

1. Join a **voice channel** or open a **DM** in Discord
2. Click the **🚀 Activity / App Launcher** icon in the channel bar
3. Find **Guess the Word** and click **Launch**
4. Start guessing — type with your keyboard or tap the on-screen keys!
